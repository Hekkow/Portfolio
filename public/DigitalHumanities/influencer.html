Sponsored influencers

Nowadays, especially after Covid, people are very attached to their favorite influencers. YouTubers in particular, there is a large focus on authenticity, and in general people trust their favorite YouTubers a lot. And as such, when they recommend that you buy a product, you're much more likely to buy the product they recommend over a competing product. Advertisers know this and spend millions of dollars sponsoring YouTubers, Twitch streamers, and oftentimes even Tiktokers, and it's extremely effective.
